About the Scores

  • Standardized Test Score: Represents performance on standardized tests (SAT, ACT, etc.) and is often correlated with cognitive abilities.
  • Memory Test Score: Measures your ability to remember and recall information.
  • Problem Solving Score: Assesses your problem-solving skills and logical reasoning.
  • Verbal Reasoning Score: Tests your ability to understand and reason using concepts framed in words.
  • Math Reasoning Score: Measures your ability to solve problems and reason using math-based concepts.
  • Processing Speed Score: Evaluates how quickly you can process information and react.
  • Spatial Reasoning Score: Tests your ability to visualize and manipulate objects in space.
